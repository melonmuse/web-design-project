Our Story

A small table, a big dream, and an unwavering belief that every meal should feel like a celebration.

Where It Began

Shofuku started with seven dishes and six drinks. Our initial menu was lean but perfectly curated — every item chosen with intention, every flavour considered from first bite to last.

The name Shofuku (笑福) means "laughing fortune" — a wish for joy at the table and good things to come. It's a philosophy we carry into everything we do.

Our Commitment

Our offerings have grown a great deal since those early days, but our commitment to perfection remains unchanged. We source carefully, cook with patience, and serve with pride.

Every dish on our menu is a product of that same care — rooted in Japanese culinary tradition, refined by years of practice, and made with the hope that it brings a smile to your face.
